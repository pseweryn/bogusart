My name is Zbigniew Boguslawski, I am an illustrator, painter, graphic designer...

My main area is traditional graphics – drawing (ink, pencil, charcoal) etching, silkscreen, linocut, and woodcut but extends into photography, computer graphics, painting and sculpture. My works have been published in various independent Polish magazines (such as Blok, Syndrom, Pole, and Cracow Comics Club), as well as some mainstream periodicals (Arlezine, Czachopismo, Stoke Newington Gazette). As a freelancer I made designs for CD, LP covers, book illustration, t-shirts, tattoos, drawn comics painted murals and created many oil and acrylic paintings.

My diploma work in linocut has been presented on the website of the International Graphic Triennial in Cracow. One could see my works exhibited in Poland, Finland and UK.

For over 10 years (five years of Secondary School of Arts in Cracow, five years at the Institute of Art – part of the Pedagogical Academy of Cracow and one year at the Academy of Art & Design in Helsinki) I have been studying this subject and now I continue to work in this field.
